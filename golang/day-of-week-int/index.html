Day of week

yourbasic.org

The Weekday function returns returns the day of the week of a time.Time.

func (t Time) Weekday() Weekday

In use:

weekday := time.Now().Weekday()
fmt.Println(weekday)      // "Tuesday"
fmt.Println(int(weekday)) // "2"
